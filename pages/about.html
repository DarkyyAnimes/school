About?

Established in 2040 B.S., Budhanilkantha Secondary English School is located in Shankarpur, Biratnagar-2. The school offers education from Playgroup (PG) to Grade 10. Known for its consistent commitment to quality education, the institution has achieved academic distinction and holistic development for students across various fields. Over the years, it has gained a reputation for producing top achievers, particularly in SLC/SEE examinations, and for fostering student talents through international exposure and extracurricular excellence.

Our History

Founded in 2040 B.S., Budhanilkantha School has grown from a small community school into one of the leading educational institutions in Biratnagar. Our legacy is built on a tradition of excellence, dedicated teachers, and a supportive learning environment.

Our Values

  • Student participation in international events (e.g., NASA USA, Asian Camp in Japan).
  • Multiple-time winners in oratory and drawing competitions.
  • ICC Panel Umpire Mr. Buddhi Bahadur Pradhan guiding sports training.
  • Robotics and science exhibition winners at local and national levels.
  • Hosts facilities like:
    • Kids' Entertainment Hall and TV-based learning rooms
    • 250-seat Conference and Projector Hall
    • Filtered drinking water
    • Library, science and computer labs
    • Super learning and remedial classes
    • Hostel accommodation
    • Wide range of co-curricular activities including music, dance, cricket, football, taekwondo, and field trips.
  • Emphasis on both academic performance and practical learning approaches.
